The Healing Camp

A seaside camp where nature sets the rhythm. Wake to ocean sounds, dine on mindful meals, and let the island pace slow you down. The Healing Camp is not a resort — it's a place to rest, reconnect, and breathe.

Nestled along the coast of Cat Ba Island, The Healing Camp combines simple, comfortable camping with mindful dining and wellness-oriented experiences. Everything here is designed with restraint — fewer distractions, more presence.

Lan Ha Floating Homestay

An uncommon stay on the water. Our floating homestay sits quietly in Lan Ha Bay — surrounded by karst mountains, far from noise, close to everything that matters.
