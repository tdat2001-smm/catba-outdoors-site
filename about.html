About Cat Ba Outdoors

Off the beaten path since day one

Cat Ba Outdoors is a hospitality and lifestyle ecosystem rooted in Cat Ba Island, built around nature, sea, and a slower way of living.

We develop places to stay, eat, rest, and experience — not as isolated businesses, but as connected spaces that share the same rhythm and values. From seaside camping and mindful dining to bars, outdoor experiences, and wellness-oriented projects, everything we do is shaped by the island itself: its tides, its seasons, and its unhurried pace.

Cat Ba Outdoors is not about mass tourism. It's about creating places that feel grounded, human, and real — places people want to return to, not just visit once.

Our Philosophy

We believe good hospitality starts with restraint. Instead of expanding fast or copying trends, Cat Ba Outdoors focuses on doing fewer things — but doing them with intention, care, and long-term thinking.

Every project is designed to work with its surroundings, not against them. We prioritize flow, functionality, and sustainability in both experience and operations, ensuring that what we build can be maintained, improved, and lived in over time.

For us, growth is not about scale alone, but about depth — deeper experiences, stronger teams, and more meaningful connections with guests and the local environment.

What We Create

Across our ecosystem, guests will find spaces that encourage slowing down and staying present. Whether it's a quiet night by the sea, a slow meal shared at the table, a drink at dusk, or an outdoor gathering under the open sky — our places are designed to feel unforced and human.

Each project within Cat Ba Outdoors has its own identity, but they are all connected by the same principles: simplicity, rhythm, and respect for place. We don't aim to entertain endlessly. We aim to create the conditions for rest, connection, and a sense of belonging — even if only for a few days.
